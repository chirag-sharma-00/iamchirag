ABOUT ME

I love approaching new areas of knowledge and gaining new perspectives on things I thought I understood well. And lately, I've found no shortness of opportunity to get exposed to new experiences and to learn by doing. 

Since starting my college career, I’ve learned so much from my peers, professors, and colleagues, but there's still a whole world of new experiences out there that I'm itching to get introduced to. I've enjoyed working on ideas with others in the past and I'm excited to see what new projects could benefit from my unique skill set.

If anything you see here aligns with an area you work on or if you're looking for someone who's eager to learn and gain new skills on the job, I’d love to chat with you about how we might work together on something.
